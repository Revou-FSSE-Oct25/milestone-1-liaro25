About Me

Since 2020, I’ve been building a new chapter of life in Tokyo after relocating with my husband. As a mother of three — one autistic boy, one very chatty little girl, and one super active toddler — my days are a blend of parenting, learning, and rediscovering myself both personally and professionally.

I bring 10+ years of experience in the Mobile Telecommunications industry, covering full end-to-end application planning — from receiving hardware and installing software, to testing with the hardware division, and preparing applications for customer delivery. My work with Telkomsel, Indosat, Axis, XL, and HCPT helped build my foundation in system design, troubleshooting, and cross-functional collaboration.

Today, my love for exploring halal food leads my work with Halal Gourmet Japan, where I discover halal-friendly eateries, create content, and share insights to support Muslim residents and travelers in Japan.

I also joined Hello World Japan, taking part in English-based quest games as an Indonesian living in Japan, helping Japanese children learn English while introducing Indonesian culture. In addition, I teach weekly private Indonesian lessons to a child from an Indonesian–French family living in Tokyo.

I’m currently expanding my skills through RevoU’s Full-Stack Software Engineering program while continuing my steady Japanese-learning journey — one day and one chapter at a time.

My goal is to blend my technical background, multicultural experiences, communication skills, and my well-honed management abilities (because raising three kids in Japan is real project management!) to create meaningful, user-friendly solutions that support diverse communities.
